Viaplay is a Nordic streaming service that offers a wide range of digital content, including movies, TV shows, and live sports. It is owned by Nordic Entertainment Group (NENT Group) and is available in Nordic countries including Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, and Iceland.




How to activate Viaplay?


  1. Open the Viaplay app on your streaming device.
  2. You will get a Viaplay activation code on your TV screen.
    • (If there is any problem finding the activation code, we have given the information above.)
  3. Go to play.no/active using a mobile or PC browser.
  4. Enter the Viaplay activation code.
  5. Click Activate. Select.
  6. Enter your Viaplay login details.
  7. Then, tap SEND.
